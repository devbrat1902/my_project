Our Programs

Engaging preschool learning programs for every age group

Playgroup (18M-2Y)

Sensory-rich environment for our youngest learners. Focus on safe exploration, parent-child bonding, and foundational development.

  • Sensory Play Activities
  • Safe Exploration
  • Parent Bonding Sessions

Nursery (2Y-3Y)

Creative and interactive learning through art, music, and storytelling. Building social skills and emotional intelligence.

  • Art & Craft Activities
  • Music & Movement
  • Social Skill Development

Lower KG (3Y-4Y)

Structured learning with introduction to letters and numbers. Focus on fine motor skills and kindergarten readiness.

  • Letter Recognition
  • Number Concepts
  • Motor Skill Development

Upper KG (4Y-5Y)

School readiness program with reading, writing, and math fundamentals. Preparing children for successful transition to primary school.

  • Reading & Writing
  • Math Fundamentals
  • School Readiness

Extracurricular Activities

Specialized hobby classes in music, dance, sports, and arts. Nurturing talent and building confidence in every child.

  • Music & Dance Classes
  • Sports & Movement
  • Creative Arts Programs

Franchise Opportunities

Grow your preschool business with our proven model. Complete training, curriculum support, and ongoing mentorship provided.

  • Business Training
  • Curriculum Support
  • Quality Assurance
